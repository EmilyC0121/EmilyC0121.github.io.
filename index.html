Wildfires

"Uncontrolled fire in a forest, grassland, brushland, or land sown to crops" - Britannica

Wildfires are a direct correlation of climate change. As everyday people, like you or me, we may not be able to do much. However, we can do small things to help. Donate to organizations like California Wildfire Relief Fund or the American Red Cross. Join a club or organization that can help your voice be heard. Or send a letter to a politician to get your message spread!
